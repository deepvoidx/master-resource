🔢 How to set up free visitor analytics

Step 1: Sign up for free at goatcounter.com — no cookies, privacy-friendly, free for personal use.

Step 2: During signup, choose a site code (e.g. masterresource). Your dashboard URL will be https://masterresource.goatcounter.com

Step 3: Add the tracking script to your index.html just before </body>:

<script data-goatcounter="https://yourcode.goatcounter.com/count" async src="//gc.zgo.at/count.js"></script>

Step 4: Paste your GoatCounter dashboard URL above and click Load. You will see page views, visitor countries, referrers, and visit times — all in real time.

Note: GitHub Pages is static — no server-side analytics. GoatCounter is the best free option that actually works.
